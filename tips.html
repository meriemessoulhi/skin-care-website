Do & Don’t

✔ Do

Take care of your skin every day
Drink plenty of water
Clean your face twice a day
Use products suitable for your skin type
Apply sunscreen every morning
Moisturize your skin daily
Get enough sleep
Eat healthy food
Be gentle with your skin
Remove makeup before sleeping

❌ Don’t

Don’t sleep with makeup on
Don’t touch your face too much
Avoid using too many products
Don’t skip sunscreen
Avoid harsh chemicals
Don’t change products too often
Avoid hot water on your face
Don’t ignore your skin type
Avoid stress as much as possible
Don’t pop pimples
